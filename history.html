About

Our History

Tuisyen Anatasia was founded in 2014 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between classroom learning and individual student needs. Recognizing that every student has a unique potential, our founder, Sir Firdaus, set out to create an environment where learning is personalized, engaging, and results-driven.

The journey began with a small group of dedicated educators who shared a passion for teaching and a vision for student success. Starting with just a few students in a humble classroom, the tuition center quickly gained a reputation for its tailored approach, innovative teaching methods, and a commitment to excellence.

Over the years, Tuisyen Anatasia has grown to serve hundreds of students annually, offering courses across core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and English, as well as specialized programs for test preparation and skill enhancement.

What sets us apart is our dedication to fostering a supportive community for students. By blending traditional teaching techniques with modern technology, we ensure that learning is both effective and engaging. Our team of experienced educators works closely with students to identify their strengths and address their challenges, helping them achieve their academic goals and build confidence.

Today, Tuisyen Anatasia is a trusted name in education, celebrated for its results, values, and the success stories of its students. We remain committed to evolving and innovating to meet the changing needs of students and to empowering the next generation of learners.
